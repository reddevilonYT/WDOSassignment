SANTANI LK

At Santani LK, we guarantee that you will be in good hands. We are glad to welcome you into our beachfront resort, which is one of Sri Lanka's most prestigious hotels. We are extremely proud of our renowned hospitality, which will live on in your mind for the rest of your life.


An endless amount of leisure activities

Partake in unique experiences offered by santani. Everything from surfing the calm, gentle waves to fishing with the locals on stilts. Add a new flavor to the existing palette for adventure.

For those who prefer a less adventurous holiday could even go whale watching or take a relaxing trip to our spas , that utilize the uniqueness of Sri Lankan culture to deliver a unforgettable experience to you.


Our exclusive cuisine and accomadation


Our interiors are also created to meld the best of Sri Lankan hospitality with the natural beauty of our surroundings, creating atmospheres of hidden luxury that are difficult to rival. Your stay with us will be one to remember, with rooms that transport you to into a world of their own, and distinctive dining that transport you to a world beyond. We will always welcome you with open arms and a tropical embrace to make you feel at home, no matter how long you choose to be with our family.


Location

Located off the sandy beaches of Mirissa. Satani LK is sure to catch your eye. Satani LK is located in the Mirissa beach. Santani LK is towards the west coast of Mirissa. Road instructions after reaching Mirissa:









  1. Head east towards Colombo
  2. Turn right from the second roundabout
  3. Head 10km on that road to arrive at Santani LK
